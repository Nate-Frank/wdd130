Nutrition and Hydration in Utah

Utah might not always look like it but it is definitely a desert where temperatures can reach upwards and around 100 degrees during the hottest parts of the day so it is absolutely essential that while visiting you drink plenty of water beforehand and are constantly staying hydrated but that while exercising, mountain biking, hiking, or anything that causes additional exertion on your body that you drink the recommended amount of liquids according to your age and weight.
